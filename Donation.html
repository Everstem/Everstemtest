Donations


Benevity

Everstem is qualified to receive donations and company volunteer hour matches through benevity.org. Hundreds of companies choose Benevity to make a charitable impact, such as Microsoft, Google, Starbucks e.g.

For example, for Microsoft employee, go to https://microsoft.benevity.org, Search and select "everstem education".


AmazonSmile

Do you know that AmazonSmile can donate 0.5% of your eligible purchases to Everstem at no cost to you. To support Everstem, on your first visit to smile.amazon.com, add Everstem to receive donations. Or you can simply purchase with this link.
