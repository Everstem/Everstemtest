Everstem Education

A non-profit organization run by students for students

Everstem is a 501(c3) non-profit organization based in Washington State, USA. We are dedicated to making STEM educational opportunities more accessible for all students through free workshops, seminars, hackathon events and providing grants and mentorship for new FIRST Lego League (FLL) and FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC)


Event

We provide students stem education opportunities including free workshops, seminars and hackathons. The goal is to connect students with professionals in tech industry and help them prepare for stem related careers.

Grant

We provide grants for new Washington FIRST FLL & FTC teams, as well as offer mentorships to help establish new teams in FIRST programs.
